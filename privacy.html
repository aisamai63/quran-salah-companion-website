Overview

Quran & Salah Companion is an Islamic companion app that provides Quran reading, prayer times, qibla direction, adhkar, smart spiritual reminders, and related features.

The app is designed to minimize data collection and does not require account registration for core features.

Information the app may access

Location

The app may request access to your device location to calculate prayer times, qibla direction, and nearby masjid-related features. Location is used for app functionality and is not sold.

Notifications

The app may request notification permission to provide optional prayer notifications, smart spiritual reminders, Quran reminders, adhkar reminders, and related reminders.

Internet access

The app may use internet access for features such as Quran audio streaming, online content loading, or service updates.

Local app data

The app may store preferences locally on your device, including prayer settings, notification settings, selected reciter, last read Quran page, dhikr progress, and app preferences.

Information we do not require

The app does not require user account registration, passwords, payment information, contacts, SMS, or phone call logs.

How information is used

Information is used to calculate prayer times, show qibla direction, deliver optional reminders, remember preferences, and improve the app experience.

Data sharing

We do not sell user data. The app may communicate with third-party services only when needed for app functionality, such as audio streaming or map/location-related services.

Data retention

Local preferences and progress remain on your device until you clear app data, uninstall the app, or reset related settings if available.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and does not require account creation. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

User choices

You can deny location permission, disable notifications, clear app data through Android settings, or uninstall the app. Some features may not work fully if permissions are denied.

For privacy questions, contact abdelsalam.apps@gmail.com.
